I delivered an "IoT Solution Design" talk at The American University in Cairo as part of **Made In Egypt 2023 (16th edition)**.

## What I shared

The session focused on practical IoT architecture decisions:

- How to think about device-to-cloud flow.
- How to design for reliability, not only demos.
- How to connect technical implementation to real business value.

## What made the event valuable

Engaging with students and professionals in one room created strong technical discussions and practical questions about real-world adoption.

## Personal takeaway

Community knowledge-sharing accelerates the ecosystem. Teaching and building reinforce each other.
