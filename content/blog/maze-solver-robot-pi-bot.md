This post captures an older but meaningful project from my internship at Sector B5 in 2021.

## Project snapshot

We built a maze-solver robot. My focus was firmware and behavior logic, while my teammate handled mechanical design in SolidWorks.

## Engineering focus

The core challenge was translating algorithmic decisions into stable movement behavior under practical constraints.

That meant balancing:

- Decision correctness.
- Timing and control stability.
- Real hardware limitations.

## Why it still matters

Projects like this build the mindset needed for embedded and IoT products: tight feedback loops, careful debugging, and disciplined iteration.
