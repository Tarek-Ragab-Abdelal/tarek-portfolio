This was one of the sessions I still value deeply: an "Introduction to IoT" event at the Higher Technological Institute with IEEE HTI OCT SB.

## Why this session was important

Intro sessions are where many engineers build their first mental model of IoT.

The objective was to keep concepts accessible while staying practical, so attendees could connect theory with implementation.

## What stood out

Student energy and questions were excellent. When the audience is curious, the session becomes a shared workshop rather than a one-way presentation.

## Ongoing lesson

Technical education creates long-term leverage. Every strong intro can influence many future projects.
