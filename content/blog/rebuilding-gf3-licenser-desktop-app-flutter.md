I had the chance to collaborate again with Boreal Laser Inc. to recreate the **GF3 Licenser** desktop application.

## Project focus

The goal was to deliver a stronger desktop experience while keeping workflows clear for operational use.

## Collaboration that helped execution

This project moved smoothly because communication quality was high:

- Fast feedback cycles with the client.
- Clear decision-making around functionality.
- UI/UX support that improved implementation speed.

## Outcome

The rebuild produced a cleaner application experience and a stronger base for future feature work.

A recurring lesson from product work: technical quality and communication quality multiply each other.
